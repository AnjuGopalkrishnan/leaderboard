Why a Query combat Competition?

Query Combat is the home of SQL competitions, with over 13 million users, ready to solve your SQL problems through competitions. You set the terms, Participants construct their query, and our website scores their queries in real time based on the metric selected by you, to find the winner. Competitions are the smart, simple way to tackle a tough business problem, educate students, or simply create a fun event. You bring the problem and the data, and Query Combat brings the platform.
